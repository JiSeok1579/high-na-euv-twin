# INST-001 - Study-Grade Purpose and Severity Relaxation

Status: implemented and archived
Last updated: 2026-04-28

This instruction established that the simulator is study-grade. It is retained
for history; the active policy now lives in `PROJECT_OVERVIEW.md`,
`PROJECT_PLAN.md`, and `REVIEWER_DIRECTIVE.md`.

## Implemented Policy

- The project prioritizes learning, visualization, exploration, and paper
  traceability.
- Industrial scanner matching is out of scope.
- Missing industrial ground truth is not a P0 issue by itself.
- Broken tests, sign errors, unit confusion, false audit status, and
  documentation-code drift remain high-priority issues.
- Validation should be honest about reduced models.
