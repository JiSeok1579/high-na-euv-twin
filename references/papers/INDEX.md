## Minimum MVP Reading Set

1. Paper 19 - anamorphic High-NA optics for projection coordinates.
2. Paper 9 - fast High-NA SMO for the optimization baseline.
3. Paper 12 - mask 3D characterization for the six-effect checklist.
4. Paper 4 - CAR / PSCAR fundamentals for resist simplification.
5. Paper 14 - DOF challenge and LCDU context.

## Access Note

Open-access and free papers can be downloaded manually in a browser. Paywalled
papers require publisher, SPIE, or institutional access. Do not commit PDFs
unless licensing permits it.
